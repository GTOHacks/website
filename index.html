GTOHacks 2024

A new hackathon for students in the GTA and across Canada.

What is GTOHacks?

  • A hackathon run by high school students, for high school students
  • We aim to introduce students to the world of programming and problem-solving and allow experienced students to use their skills to solve real-world problems
  • Each year, GTOHacks offers a new and innovative theme that challenges the participant's creativity and technical skills.

Competition Format

  • 2 day format
  • Participants will use the first day to build teams and develop their ideas
  • The second day will be a cumulative presentation consisting of their vision, their process, and demonstration to our panel of judges.

The Theme

For GTOHacks 2024, our theme will be studying. Participants will create a program to assist students in their studying. Key problems surrounding this topic may include procrastination, burnout, and information retention. In the name of fairness, a detailed case will be revealed on the first day so no team has an advantage.
